1950s China-India Chronology

Methodology

This chronology was constructed using information from the following sources:

  1. Newspapers:
    1. Renmin ribao (The People’s Daily) 人民日報
    2. Guangming ribao 光明日報
    3. The Times of India
    The newspaper searches were conducted using the India-China Friendship Association (ICFA; 印中友好協會) and the China-India Friendship Association (CIFA; 中印友好協會) as principal key words.

  2. Contemporary Scholarship:
    To the newspaper results, we added information contained in contemporary secondary scholarship that focused on Sino-Indian encounters. Two prominent examples of such scholarship are:
      Passin, Herbert. 1961. “Sino-Indian Cultural Relations.” China Quarterly 7:85–100. Fisher, Margaret W., and Joan V. Bondurant. 1956. “Review Article: The Impact of Communist China on Visitors from India.” Far Eastern Quarterly 15(2):249–65.

  3. Archives and Library Collections: Finally, we added information collected from a range of archives and libraries over the course the past several years. These include: Beijing Municipal Archives, Beijing institute for the history of natural science, Chinese academy of sciences, Beijing Foreign Ministry Archives, Beijing Institute of economic growth, New Delhi National Archives of India, New Delhi National Library of China, Beijing National Library of India, Kolkata Nehru Memorial Museum and Library, New Delhi PC Mahalanobis memorial museum and archives, Kolkata Shripati Chandrasekhar Papers, Ward M. Canaday Center for Special Collections, the University of Toledo, Ohio Tagore library, university of Lucknow, Lucknow Tata institute of fundamental research archives, mumbai
